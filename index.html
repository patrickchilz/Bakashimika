Welcome to Bakashimika

The International Photography Festival

Festival is an annual photography festival which celebrates the rich photographic community in Zambia and the Southern African region. The festival provides a platform for emerging southern African photographers, who tell new and innovative African stories through photography and lens-based art.

Festival brings together the wealth of photographic practices in Zambia and the region and raises awareness of the existing photographic talent.
The festival gives a platform to the visual storytellers and their contemporary stories found within the vibrant and visually exciting photographic arts community of Southern African.
